Dr. Vipin Chaudhary is a Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at the University at Buffalo, SUNY, where he directs the New York State Center of Excellence in Bioinformatics and Life Sciences and the Data Intensive Discovery Initiative. He previously served at Wayne State University, where he was the Director of the Institute for Scientific Computing and held multiple professorships. He still maintains an adjunct position in Neurological Surgery at Wayne State.

Expertise:High Performance Computing and Applications to Science, Engineering, Biology, and Medicine; Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning/Data Science; Computer Assisted Diagnosis and Interventions; Medical Image Processing; Computer Architecture; Quantum Computing.
